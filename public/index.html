Rules

Each round everyone will have the same topic and category. Out of all of the players (a minimum of 3 players), there will be one Outcast. The Outcast will have a different topic but same category.

Every player will ask a question (must end with a question mark) about their topic. Then, each person will have to answer a question that was submitted by someone else.

After everyone answers a question, they will be at a display with everyone's questions and answers. Each player can only see their question asked and own answer along with the name of the person who answered their question.
The whole group must find out who the Outcast is. That is, whoever's answer or question seems the most suspicious. After choosing the Outcast, all the players press "End Round" to display who the Outcast was.
